About Us

The American Beekeeping Federation

The ABF is a national organization with over 1,200 members that continually works in the interest of all beekeepers, large or small, and those associated with the industry to ensure the future of the honey bee. Our members share a common interest to work toward better education and information for all segments of the industry in the hope of increasing our chances for survival in today's competitive world.

For more information about membership in the ABF, please visit the Member Benefits and Member Types and Dues Schedule pages of this Web site or join now!
